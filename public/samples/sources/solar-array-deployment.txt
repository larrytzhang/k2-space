PROCEDURE: SOLAR ARRAY DEPLOYMENT VERIFICATION TEST
Document No: SAT-TEST-2041
Revision: C
Effective Date: 2026-02-14

PURPOSE
This procedure verifies the nominal deployment kinematics and end-of-travel latching of
the primary solar array wing assembly prior to spacecraft integration.

SCOPE
Applies to all flight-configuration solar array wings (P/N 40127-FLT). Does not apply to
qualification or engineering-model hardware.

ROLES
Test Conductor (TC)
Quality Assurance (QA)
Mechanical Engineer (ME)
Safety Officer (SO)

EQUIPMENT
Torque Wrench, 0–5 N·m, calibrated (Qty: 1)
Deployment Fixture SAF-400 (Qty: 1)
High-speed camera, 240 fps minimum (Qty: 2)
Nitrogen purge cart, regulated 2–5 psi (Qty: 1)
ESD-safe gloves (Qty: as required)

1. PRE-DEPLOYMENT CHECKS

  WARNING: Array panels store significant strain energy in the stowed configuration.
  Release of restraint cables without proper fixture engagement can cause uncontrolled
  deployment and serious injury. Confirm Safety Officer presence before proceeding.

  1.1 Verify that the deployment fixture SAF-400 is rigidly bolted to the test table.
      Torque mounting bolts to 4.5 N·m.
      Torque reading: _____ N·m
      TC signature: _____________

  1.2 CAUTION: Do not energize the release mechanism until nitrogen purge is active.
      Electrostatic discharge may damage the pyrotechnic initiator.

  1.3 Enable nitrogen purge at 3.0 ± 0.5 psi. Verify flow on cart flowmeter.
      Observed purge pressure: _____ psi
      Pass / Fail: _____
      ME signature: _____________

  1.4 NOTE: The high-speed cameras must be triggered at T-5 seconds to ensure pre-trigger
      data capture.

2. RELEASE AND DEPLOYMENT

  2.1 Arm release mechanism. Confirm arming indicator is GREEN.
      Indicator state: ___ GREEN ___ RED
      QA signature: _____________

  2.2 Initiate T-10 second countdown. Trigger high-speed cameras at T-5.

  2.3 Issue release command. Observe deployment.

    2.3.1 WARNING: Maintain personnel exclusion zone of 3 meters during deployment.
          No personnel within deployment envelope.

    2.3.2 Time to full deployment: _____ seconds (acceptance: 4.5–6.0 s)
          Pass / Fail: _____

    2.3.3 Verify all four latch indicators show LATCHED.
          Latch 1: ___ LATCHED ___ NOT LATCHED
          Latch 2: ___ LATCHED ___ NOT LATCHED
          Latch 3: ___ LATCHED ___ NOT LATCHED
          Latch 4: ___ LATCHED ___ NOT LATCHED

3. POST-DEPLOYMENT INSPECTION

  3.1 Visually inspect hinge lines for binding, scoring, or debris.
      Pass / Fail: _____
      ME signature: _____________

  3.2 Measure final tip displacement from reference datum.
      Measurement: _____ mm (acceptance: 4020 ± 15 mm)
      Pass / Fail: _____

  3.3 Disable nitrogen purge.

  3.4 Record test complete.
      TC signature: _____________
      QA signature: _____________
      Date: _____________

ESTIMATED DURATION: 90 minutes
